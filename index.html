ABOUT US

Company History: Established in Chennai, capital of Tamilnadu state, India in the year of 1997. Priya Constructions began as a civil work contractor for Residential & Industrial constructions. Over the years the company has undertaken many projects and accumulated skills, know –how and experience in design and build solutions and related fabrication works. Now, Priya Constructions becomes a reputed construction contractor for small and medium scale projects. We ‘Priya Constructions’ are engaged in providing various kind of construction services begins from Design, Estimation, constructions works includes, basic civil constructions, related fabrication works, Industrial roads, Electrical work contracts, Drainage & Rainwater Harvesting, Fire hydrant network and Horticulture services. Our object is to provide our clients to an elegant completion of their projects and delivered an assured experience when we are chosen to execute. We use high quality raw materials from quality driven suppliers/manufacturers that is confirmed by our quality assurance team. We are one of the quality centric organization and our motto is ‘Quality is Real Success’.

Our Vision: To be a genuine construction company delivering the best beyond expectations, always.

Our Mission: To deliver the projects on time at a competitive price with quality materials and workmanship and ensure a safe workplace and environmental responsibilities.
